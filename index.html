Sales Samples

How I think through outbound sales

Cold Call Opener

“Hey [Name], this is Paul. I know I’m calling out of the blue — do you have 20 seconds for me to explain why I called, and then you can tell me if it’s worth continuing?”

“Send Me an Email”

“Absolutely. So I don’t send something irrelevant, what should I focus the email on — saving time, lowering costs, or improving your team’s current process?”

Discovery Questions

  • How are you handling this process today?
  • What is working well with your current approach?
  • What would need to improve for it to be worth changing?
